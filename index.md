## about me
i'm a first-year phd student at the [IMDEA Software Institute](https://software.imdea.org/), working under the supervision of [Aleks Nanevski](https://software.imdea.org/~aleks/).

previously, i obtained a master's degree in formal methods in computer science from [Universidad Complutense de Madrid](https://www.ucm.es) and [Universidad Politécnica de Madrid](https://www.upm.es/), a master's degree in mathematics from [Universidad de Sevilla](https://www.us.es/), and a bachelor's degree in computer science from [Universidad Centroamericana José Simeón Cañas](https://uca.edu.sv/).

my research interests include proof theory, type theory and programming language semantics.
