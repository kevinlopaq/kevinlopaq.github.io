## About me
I'm a first-year PhD student at the [IMDEA Software Institute](https://software.imdea.org/), working under the supervision of [Aleks Nanevski](https://software.imdea.org/~aleks/).

Previously, I obtained a master's degree in formal methods in computer science from [Universidad Complutense de Madrid](https://www.ucm.es) and [Universidad Politécnica de Madrid](https://www.upm.es/), a master's in mathematics from [Universidad de Sevilla](https://www.us.es/), and a bachelor's in computer science from [Universidad Centroamericana José Simeón Cañas](https://uca.edu.sv/).

My research interests include proof theory, type theory and programming language semantics.
